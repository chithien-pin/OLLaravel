Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Introduction

GYPSYLIVE ("we", "us", "our") operates a social dating and live streaming mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use and protect it, and your rights regarding your data.

By creating an account or using GYPSYLIVE, you agree to the collection and use of your data as described in this policy. You may withdraw your consent or change your preferences at any time through your account settings.

Our Commitment We will never sell your personal information to third parties. We only share data with service providers who are necessary to operate our platform, and we require them to protect your data.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you create an account, set up your profile, or use our features, you provide us with:

  • Account information: name, email address, phone number, date of birth, gender, password
  • Profile information: photos, bio, interests, hobbies, and dating preferences
  • Content you create: posts, stories, comments, messages, and live stream recordings
  • Verification data: identity documents submitted for profile verification
  • Payment information: transaction details when purchasing Diamonds (processed by third-party payment providers â€” we do not store your card details)

Information Collected Automatically

When you use GYPSYLIVE, we automatically collect:

  • Device information: device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, push notification tokens
  • Location data: GPS coordinates (when you grant location permission) used for user discovery and matching
  • IP address and approximate location derived from your network connection
  • Usage data: features you interact with, time spent on the app, swipe activity, and content engagement

Sensitive Personal Data

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data such as health information, religious beliefs, or political affiliations. If you choose to include such information in your profile or posts, you do so voluntarily and it will be visible to other users according to your privacy settings.

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Provide core services: user matching, profile discovery, messaging, social feed, and live streaming
  • Personalize your experience: recommend profiles, suggest content, and tailor your discovery feed based on your preferences and location
  • Process transactions: handle Diamond purchases, gift transfers between users, and streamer redemption requests
  • Communicate with you: send push notifications for matches, messages, likes, comments, and system updates
  • Ensure safety: detect and prevent fraud, fake accounts, abuse, and violations of our Terms of Use
  • Improve our services: analyze usage patterns to enhance features, fix bugs, and optimize performance
  • Comply with legal obligations: respond to lawful requests from authorities when required

Data Sharing & Third-Party Services

We share your data only when necessary to provide our services. Specifically:

  • Other users: your profile information, posts, stories, and live streams are visible to other users based on your privacy settings
  • Live streaming: Agora (video streaming infrastructure) processes audio/video data during live sessions
  • Push notifications: Firebase Cloud Messaging delivers notifications to your device
  • Payments: Stripe and Apple/Google in-app purchase systems process your transactions
  • Cloud storage: your uploaded media (photos, videos) is stored on secure cloud servers
  • Analytics: we use analytics tools to understand app usage and improve our services
We will never sell your personal data or contact information to advertisers or other third parties. Our service providers are contractually required to protect your data and may only use it to provide services on our behalf.

Location Data

GYPSYLIVE uses your location to enable core features of the app:

  • Show nearby users on the discovery map
  • Match you with users based on geographic proximity
  • Display approximate distance on user profiles

You can disable location access through your device settings at any time. Disabling location may limit the functionality of discovery and matching features.

Data Protection & Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS) for all communications
  • Secure server infrastructure with access controls
  • Token-based authentication for API access
  • Regular security reviews and monitoring
Payment Security All payment transactions are processed through PCI-compliant third-party providers (Stripe, Apple App Store, Google Play Store). We never store your credit card numbers or bank account details on our servers.

Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your personal data only for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. Specifically:

  • Account data: retained while your account is active
  • Messages and chat history: retained while both users' accounts are active
  • Live stream data: session metadata is retained for earnings and analytics purposes
  • Transaction records: retained as required by applicable financial regulations
When you delete your account, we will permanently delete your personal data, profile, posts, and media within 30 days. Some data may be retained longer if required by law (e.g., financial transaction records).

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Objection: opt out of marketing communications at any time via account settings
  • Portability: request your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent: revoke permissions (location, notifications) through your device settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@vazdino.com. We will respond within 30 days. If you are not satisfied with our response, you may contact your local data protection authority.

Marketing & Notifications

We may send you push notifications about new matches, messages, likes, and app updates. You can manage notification preferences in your account settings or disable them through your device settings at any time.

We may occasionally send promotional communications about new features or offers. You can opt out of marketing messages through your account settings.

Children's Privacy

GYPSYLIVE is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18 years of age. If we discover that a user is under 18, we will immediately terminate their account and delete their data.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country of residence. We ensure that any international transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The current version will always be available in the app. We will notify you of significant changes through in-app notifications or your registered email address.
